Nicole Cahoon CPA

Nicole Cahoon, CPA offers a full range of accounting and bookkeeping services to small and mid-sized businesses in Courtenay, Comox, Cumberland and all neighbouring towns in the Comox Valley.

At Nicole Cahoon, CPA we are committed to provide a variety of services to meet the needs of our clients by fostering an environment that encourages personal and professional growth. We are dedicated to provide excellent customer service by focusing on the three underlying principles of professionalism, responsiveness and quality.
